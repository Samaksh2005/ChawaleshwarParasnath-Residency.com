Hostel Rules & Regulations

General Guidelines

  • All residents must carry their hostel ID at all times.
  • Respect for fellow residents, staff, and property is expected at all times.
  • Hostel rooms and common areas must be kept clean and tidy.

Entry & Exit Timings

  • Entry into the hostel premises is allowed until 10:00 PM.
  • Late entry requires prior permission from the warden or management.
  • Visitors are allowed only in designated areas during visiting hours (10:00 AM â€“ 6:00 PM).

Room Usage

  • Cooking inside rooms is strictly prohibited.
  • Furniture and electrical appliances must not be removed or altered.
  • Any damage to hostel property must be reported and will be charged to the resident.

Prohibited Activities

  • Smoking, drinking, or use of any intoxicants is strictly forbidden.
  • Unauthorized guests or overnight stays are not allowed.
  • Playing loud music or causing disturbance is not permitted.

Disciplinary Action

  • Violation of rules may lead to warnings, fines, or expulsion from the hostel.
  • Management reserves the right to take appropriate action in case of misconduct.
